Our Story

From Karachi to All of Pakistan

SCL Insurance started in 2010 as a small office in Karachi's Saddar district. Frustrated with the maze of paperwork and unclear pricing in the local insurance market, our founders set out to build something simpler.

Today, SCL is one of Pakistan's leading insurance comparison and brokerage platforms — partnered with EFU, Adamjee, Jubilee and TPL Direct to bring fair, transparent vehicle coverage to over 50,000 drivers nationwide.

Coverage Options

Understanding Your Coverage

Total transparency. Here's what each coverage type means for you.

Third-Party Liability

Covers damages and injuries you cause to others. Required by law for all vehicles in Pakistan.

Collision Coverage

Pays for repairs to your own vehicle after an accident, regardless of who is at fault.

Comprehensive Cover

Protects against theft, snatching, riots, floods, fire and natural disasters.

Uninsured Motorist

Protection if you're hit by a driver without valid insurance — common on Pakistani roads.

Personal Injury

Medical expenses for you and your passengers, regardless of who caused the accident.

Roadside Assistance

24/7 towing, flat tire, battery jump and lockout help — anywhere in Pakistan.
